Greatest Cars In History

Explore some of the most iconic performance cars ever created.

BMW E92 M3

The BMW E92 M3 is considered one of the greatest driver's cars ever made. It was the last M3 to use a naturally aspirated V8 engine, producing an incredible sound and delivering a unique driving experience.

  • Engine: 4.0L V8
  • Horsepower: 414 HP
  • Top Speed: 192 MPH
  • Production: 2007-2013

Porsche 911 GT3 RS

The Porsche 911 GT3 RS is one of the most respected track-focused cars in the world. Famous for its naturally aspirated engine, aerodynamic design and racing technology.

  • Engine: 4.0L Flat-Six
  • Horsepower: 518 HP
  • Top Speed: 184 MPH
  • Production: 2015-Present

Koenigsegg Agera RS

The Koenigsegg Agera RS is a Swedish hypercar that pushed the boundaries of speed and engineering. It became one of the fastest production cars ever built.

  • Engine: 5.0L Twin Turbo V8
  • Horsepower: 1,160 HP
  • Top Speed: 277 MPH
  • Production: 2015-2018

Ferrari F40

The Ferrari F40 was the final Ferrari approved by Enzo Ferrari himself. It became famous for its raw performance, lightweight design and racing-inspired DNA.

  • Engine: 2.9L Twin Turbo V8
  • Horsepower: 471 HP
  • Top Speed: 201 MPH
  • Production: 1987-1992

McLaren F1

The McLaren F1 is widely regarded as one of the greatest supercars ever created. With its central driving position and BMW V12 engine, it changed the future of performance cars.

  • Engine: 6.1L BMW V12
  • Horsepower: 618 HP
  • Top Speed: 240 MPH
  • Production: 1992-1998
